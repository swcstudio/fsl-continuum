## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Pareto Operations Templates
The system SHALL create Pareto operation templates for all major operation categories.

#### Scenario: Information Management Templates
- **WHEN** information management templates are created
- **THEN** extract, filter, prioritize, and group operations SHALL be defined
- **AND** XML tag mappings SHALL be established

#### Scenario: Field Operations Templates
- **WHEN** field operations templates are created
- **THEN** attractor, boundary, resonance, and residue operations SHALL be defined
- **AND** quantum enhancement options SHALL be included

### Requirement: Operation Execution Engine
The system SHALL implement a Pareto operations execution engine with performance optimization.

#### Scenario: Operation Processing
- **WHEN** Pareto operations are executed
- **THEN** the engine SHALL process syntax and parameters
- **AND** results SHALL be formatted according to specifications

#### Scenario: Performance Optimization
- **WHEN** operations are executed
- **THEN** the engine SHALL apply optimization strategies
- **AND** parallel processing SHALL be utilized when possible

### Requirement: Integration with BAML
The system SHALL integrate Pareto operations with BAML processing workflow.

#### Scenario: BAML-Pareto Integration
- **WHEN** BAML processing requires field operations
- **THEN** the system SHALL seamlessly invoke Pareto operations
- **AND** results SHALL be integrated into BAML output

#### Scenario: Processing Pipeline
- **WHEN** complex processing pipelines are created
- **THEN** BAML and Pareto operations SHALL be chained effectively
- **AND** pipeline performance SHALL be optimized

### Requirement: Template Management
The system SHALL manage Pareto operation templates with versioning and validation.

#### Scenario: Template Versioning
- **WHEN** templates are updated
- **THEN** version history SHALL be maintained
- **AND** backward compatibility SHALL be preserved

#### Scenario: Template Validation
- **WHEN** templates are created or modified
- **THEN** syntax and structure validation SHALL be performed
- **AND** compliance with Pareto-lang v1.0 SHALL be verified

### Requirement: Performance Benchmarking
The system SHALL benchmark Pareto operations against performance targets.

#### Scenario: Benchmark Execution
- **WHEN** performance benchmarks are run
- **THEN** operation execution time SHALL be measured
- **AND** results SHALL be compared against targets

#### Scenario: Optimization Analysis
- **WHEN** performance analysis is conducted
- **THEN** bottlenecks SHALL be identified
- **AND** optimization strategies SHALL be recommended
